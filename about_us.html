Nathan Blecharczyk (Co founder)

Nathan "Nate" Blecharczyk is an American business executive and entrepreneur.

He is the co-founder and chief strategy officer of Airbnb and Chairman of Airbnb China. Blecharczyk was also the company's first chief technology officer.

Brian Joseph Chesky (CEO)

Brian Joseph Chesky is an American Internet entrepreneur who co-founded the hospitality exchange service Airbnb.

Chesky is the CEO of the company and was named one of Time's "100 Most Influential People of 2015".
